# AI Usage

We have used Gemini in form of Antigravity, Gemini CLI, Gemini Web, ChatGPT and Claude Code.

We used it for finding vulnerabilities and making major reworks for the app. It was also used for explaining code and making certain decisions in design and on what tooling to use.
AI also adviced on how to test changes for verification.
It discovered some neat edgecases and was always there as a coding companion for faster coding.
